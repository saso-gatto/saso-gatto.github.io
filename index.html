Salvatore Gatto

Welcome to my academic portfolio website. I am an Accessibility Researcher and PhD Candidate in Brain, Mind and Computer Science at the University of Padua. My work focuses on accessibility by design, accessible web and mobile systems, assistive technologies, and inclusive digital experiences.

My current research investigates how accessibility can be integrated into software development workflows and digital content production, with particular interest in WCAG 2.2, accessibility auditing, LLM-assisted accessibility tools, and multisensory access to STEM content.
