About

I am an Accessibility Researcher and PhD Candidate in Brain, Mind and Computer Science at the University of Padua. My research focuses on accessibility by design, accessible web and mobile systems, and multisensory access to digital content. I work on integrating accessibility into software workflows and on tools that support accessible web content and auditing.

Research interests

  • Accessibility
  • Inclusive design
  • Human-computer interaction
